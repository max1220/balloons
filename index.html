About

Try to click all the balloons to get to the top of the highscore!
Collect special balloons for cool effects!
Don't click the bombs though, you'll lose if you do.

This game was written in Javascript by max1220(github) and Befrog(github).
All graphics were created in mspaint, audio files were created in audacity.
